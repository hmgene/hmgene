## Bioinformatics Scientist | Computational Biology Consultant

I develop **AI-driven computational frameworks for genomic and transcriptomic data** to study how biological information encoded in DNA is interpreted, regulated, and executed within living systems.  

My broader interest lies in the **philosophy of information and computation in biology**—understanding how molecular programs are written in genetic code, how regulatory networks process that information, and how these principles parallel the way **AI systems and machines are designed, operate, and evolve through interactions between humans and autonomous agents**.

**Focus Areas**
- AI-driven sequence modeling
- Gene regulatory networks (GRN)
- 3D genomics and gene regulation
- RNA processing: polymerase II dynamics, alternative splicing (AS) & alternative polyadenylation (APA)
- Single-cell genomics
- Lung repair and regeneration biology
- Molecular Mechanisms of Neural Development and Disease
